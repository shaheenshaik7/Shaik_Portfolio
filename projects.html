Vendor Vault

A vendor vault typically refers to a secure storage system or platform used by organizations to manage and protect sensitive information related to their vendors or suppliers. This can include contracts, compliance documents, financial records, and other critical data. The purpose of a vendor vault is to ensure that this information is easily accessible to authorized personnel while maintaining strict security measures to prevent unauthorized access.
